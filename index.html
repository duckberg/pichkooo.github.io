Share Quotes with ease

Share the quote with your friends and dear ones in just one click.

Each quote is worth reading. The one which relates to your daily life, you feed like to share with your friends and dear ones, just click Share button and you are done.

You can share your favourite quote on Instagram, Facebook, Twitter or WhatsApp. Just pick the app and choose the contact with whom you would like to share it. Funny jokes are worth sharing to make the communication lighter :-).

Enjoy quotes in your language

Pick your language to view quotes.

Enjoy reading content in your own language. Pichkoo has English, Gujarati, Hindi and Marathi languages in which quotes published. All quotes are unique per language.

The best part is you can choose multiple languages to view quote categories. You can bookmark the quote from any language. You can view bookmarked quotes irrespective of language.

Download the quote to Gallery

You can download the quote card. Use it as a screensaver or home page of your device.

Download feature is the same as downloading image or any media file. You can use the downloaded quote card as a phone's home page, screensaver or any app's profile picture and so on.
